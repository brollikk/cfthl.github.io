About Us

This is a project for the homeless that involves crypto. It is as simple as it sounds and it does make a big difference to those in need.

If you would like to be part of the mission we need Volunteers to help out with buying and handing out food in other cities around the country – we will reimburse. Even bigger plus if you are willing to accept crypto currency! Main requirement is that you take pictures and also use a provided flyer for the purpose of expanding this project.

Gallery

The lives we have touched.

Camden NJ day 1, overall day #19
June 19, 2019. Got some pretzels and water for the homeless in Camden NJ. I used to go to school in this area so I decided to go here today. The area looks improved but still full of homeless people. Next time I will bring enough for more than just 2 people.
San Francisco Day #1 – OVERALL DAY 18
June 16, 2019. Today I am sharing with you the pictures sent to us by a gracious volunteer in San Francisco. He describes the situation there as the worst he’s ever seen in the country. I believe it for sure – the homeless population density is extremely high in that area. He decided to use Amazon Go for the food and split it up for three groups/people. An amazing example of what can be done if we work together! Reimbursed in BTC as well!!!!!
Philadelphia day #11
June 11, 2019. Decided to go with the pretzel factory today. They actually give quite a lot for the money, and in terms of calories it is pretty good for the whole day. Got water as per recommendation this time as well. There was a couple living under a bridge which was a first for me. They had some belongings, but the conditions in general were rough. I hope this made their day better.
Philadelphia day #10 (Day #17 overall)
June 10, 2019. Quick handout after a long day at clinic. Gave out two meals but the second homeless person did not want to be photographed. He even tried to return the meal which I just left with him of course.
Australia Day #1 (Overall day 16)
June 5, 2019. The growth is happening! Australia day #1 has been completed by a generous volunteer. I am very happy to see that what started out as one person handing out food in one city turned into something much more. This is only the beginning – REACH OUT AND VOLUNTEER FOR YOUR OWN CITY!!!!
TORONTO (Canada!) Day 1
June 3, 2019. I’m beyond thrilled to see this project picking up some steam. A really enthusiastic volunteer contacted me last week and we made arrangements. She went out in Toronto yesterday and handed out some home-made egg salad/sandwiches. Check out the pictures! And do NOT hesitate to contact me regarding volunteering like this.
Philadelphia day 8
This was the first time I was able to prepare something at home from groceries, and actually wake up in time to successfully have everything ready before even going into the city. I was able to find 3 homeless people today, but the last one didn’t want to get photographed (completely understandable). I’m glad to be prepared, and also to minimize the costs of the meals significantly. I’ll be able to give out a ton more meals if I go this route.
Los Angeles Day 2
May 27, 2019. Overall, this is our 12th day out. Our L.A. volunteer decided to go out once more and this time chose to donate some checkers food to the homeless. Just one meal but hey, every single meal counts. Don’t forget folks, this entire project is about spreading an IDEA. This isn’t just about feeding a few homeless people, but rather reminding each other that we are all human.
Philadelphia day 7 (AND OVERALL DAY 10!!!!!!)
Today I went to subway after my clinic time was over. Meatball subs, fully loaded. Don’t really have to say more than that. These homeless guys definitely ate better than me today – and maybe it should be that way always.
